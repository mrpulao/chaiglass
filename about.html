Hello! We are the chai glass

Be part of an iconic brand with a proven track record already operating a successful franchise model. Our aim is to support you all the way to ensure you fit seamlessly into the The Chai Spot system making sure your business is running smoothly and efficiently. We will look after all the supply of products, marketing, operations and help you with the store management.

Authentic delight

The Chai Glass is the leading Chai Brand in India today which has balanced tradition with the growing consumer need for aspirational fresh food with a story. What started as an eatery paying homage to traditional Indian street food has now become an iconic brand with a real standing within every community we serve. In the same way the original The Chai Glass from the streets of India was the epitome of the community he or she served, our brand lives for the community we serve. A place in the community like its humble predecessor for chaii and conversation.
